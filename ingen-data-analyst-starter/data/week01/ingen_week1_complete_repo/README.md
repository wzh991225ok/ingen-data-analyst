## What this folder contains

This repository contains Week 1 deliverables for the inGen Dynamics Data Analyst Internship open-source work plan.
The work is based only on public information, official company materials, and open-source tools. No internal inGen systems, proprietary datasets, customer data, or confidential production data were used.

## Week 1 deliverables included

- `data/week01/product_profiles.xlsx` - product profile pack covering Fari, Senpai, Sentinel Prime AI, Aido Rover, Aido Humanoid, and Origami AI / PIC 2.0.
- `data/week01/competitor_landscape.xlsx` - 40-company service robotics landscape across five verticals.
- `data/week01/data_dictionary.xlsx` - data source inventory and source governance tracker.
- `reports/week01/product_profiles.pdf` - one-page product profile report for the six inGen product/platform areas.
- `reports/week01/robotics_landscape_report.pdf` - Week 1 landscape synthesis and observations.
- `notebooks/week01_market_mapping.ipynb` - reproducible notebook scaffold for loading and summarizing the Week 1 data files.
- `docs/data_standards.md` - initial data standards for naming, dates, source tracking, and reuse notes.
- `reports/week01/status.md` - five-line Week 1 status update.

## Tool stack

Python, pandas, Excel/OpenPyXL, Jupyter, Markdown, GitHub. Later weeks will add DuckDB, SQL, Tableau Public, Power BI, Looker Studio, forecasting, and anomaly-detection tools.

## Caution

Competitor funding and product maturity fields are based on public information and should be treated as directional. Any investment, partnership, or executive decision should verify figures from primary company materials, filings, or direct company sources.
